There are a lot of amazing women in technology and I wish I can talk about all of them but sadly there are too many for me to talk about all of them. So I narrowed it down to four specific women who I feel like should be praised graciously for the work they did.

The first one that I want to talk about is Ada Lovelace, because she created coding. She is considered to be the first computer programmer because even though she never coded, she came up with the algorithm for it. She proved that women aren't just babymakers, cooks, and cleaners. She inspired me to go into computer programming. The second one that I want to talk about is Mary jackson. She was the first African-American woman at NASA. She was an aeronautical engineer, she did work on humans. She inspired me because she proved that a woman of my color can be in technology. The next one that I want to talk about is Katherine Johnson. She did complex manual calculations, these helped people who were going to the moon, or the project mercury space flights, her work helped develop the space shuttle program. She inspired me for the same reason Mary Jackson did. She was a woman of my color working in the technology field. The last one that I want to talk about is Elizabeth Feinler. She was a biochemist who went into working with large data applications. She inspired me because even though a man created the internet, Her work helped him create the internet.
